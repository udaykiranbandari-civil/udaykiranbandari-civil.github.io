From IS to EN

Visual summary — IS 456 vs Eurocode 2

Summary

This project compares design from Indian Standard IS 456 and Eurocode 2 for a simple reinforced concrete beam and verifies results with Abaqus. Manual design and FEA show closely matching outcomes for the studied case.

Beam at a glance

Span (L)
4000 mm
Section
230 × 300 mm
Load
UDL = 15 kN/m
Materials
Concrete: M25 · Steel: Fe500

Manual vs Eurocode (simple)

  • IS 456: required steel ≈ 285 mm²
  • Eurocode 2: required steel ≈ 274 mm²
  • Provided: 2×16 mm = 402 mm² (conservative)

Try this demo

Place a ruler on two supports and add a small weight in the middle. The bend you see is what FEA predicts — small but measurable.
