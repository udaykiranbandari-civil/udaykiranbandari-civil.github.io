Research Philosophy

I believe structural engineering is not the application of clauses, but the interpretation of structural behaviour. Codes are tools — not answers.

My approach emphasizes:

  • Understanding assumptions behind code equations
  • Verifying analytical design through numerical simulation
  • Interpreting results using mechanics, not software output
