Who I Am

I am a civil engineering graduate driven by a deep curiosity for structural behaviour and the mechanics behind design codes. My academic approach emphasizes verification, reasoning, and engineering judgement.

Rather than applying formulas mechanically, I focus on understanding assumptions, limitations, and real structural response.

Research Philosophy

My work is grounded in verification-based engineering — analytical design supported by numerical simulation to validate assumptions and behaviour.

I am particularly interested in IS-to-Eurocode transition, partial safety factors, serviceability behaviour, and stress distribution.
