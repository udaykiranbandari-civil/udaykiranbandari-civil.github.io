Academic Profile

I am a Civil Engineering graduate and Gold Medalist with a strong academic focus on computational structural engineering. My undergraduate training emphasized analytical design, which I have extended toward numerical verification and finite element modeling.

My academic interest lies in the transition from Indian Standard (IS) based design to European Eurocode-oriented structural analysis, with particular emphasis on reinforced concrete behavior, serviceability performance, and numerical validation using Abaqus.

Research Work

From IS to EN — Comparative RC Beam Design and FEA Verification

This study investigates a singly reinforced concrete beam designed using IS 456:2000 and Eurocode 2 (EN 1992-1-1), followed by linear elastic finite element verification in Abaqus.

Methodology

  • Manual design using IS 456 provisions
  • Equivalent Eurocode 2 design using partial safety factors
  • 3D solid modeling and meshing in Abaqus
  • Comparison of deflection and stress response
