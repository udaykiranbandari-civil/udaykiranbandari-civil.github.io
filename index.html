About Me

I am a Civil Engineering graduate with a strong academic foundation and a disciplined, research oriented mindset. Alongside engineering, I work as a Mathematics teacher, which has strengthened my analytical thinking, conceptual clarity, and logical reasoning.

My academic focus lies in structural behaviour, codal philosophy, and verification based design, combining manual calculations with numerical analysis tools.

Independent Research on IS and Eurocode Design

This research examined the design of a reinforced concrete beam using Indian Standard IS 456 and Eurocode 2, followed by finite element verification using Abaqus. Manual design focused on flexural strength, serviceability, and reinforcement detailing, while numerical analysis validated stress distribution and deflection behaviour.
