Research, Seminar, Projects and Internship

RC Beam Design Using Indian and European Codes

This research compared reinforced concrete beam design using IS 456 and Eurocode 2. The objective was to understand differences in partial safety factors, serviceability requirements, and design philosophy.

Manual calculations were verified using finite element modelling in Abaqus, which helped study stress distribution and validate analytical assumptions. This work directly connects Indian education with European structural standards.

Overflow Detection in Drainage Systems

This project addressed urban drainage overflow using a sensor based monitoring system. The aim was early detection of overflow conditions to support better drainage management.

The project was presented as a technical poster and received second prize. It improved my applied problem solving skills and technical communication.

Seminar on Sustainable Construction Materials

This seminar studied the use of recycled aggregates, bamboo, timber, and other renewable materials in construction.

The work focused on sustainability, material behaviour, advantages, and limitations of alternative construction materials.

Extensive Survey Project

A detailed field survey project covering old tank survey, new tank survey, highway alignment, town planning layouts, and water supply systems.

The project involved accurate field measurements, data processing, and preparation of final drawings, providing strong practical exposure.

Industry Internship at Medini Technologies

A fifteen week internship focused on AutoCAD drafting, BIM workflows, and professional documentation standards used in real projects.
