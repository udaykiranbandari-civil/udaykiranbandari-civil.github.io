Research, Projects and Internship

From Indian Standards to Eurocodes in Reinforced Concrete Design

This independent research compares IS 456 and Eurocode 2 for reinforced concrete beam design. The work focuses on differences in partial safety factors, serviceability checks, stress limits, and design philosophy.

Manual calculations were supported with finite element modelling in Abaqus to study stress distribution and validate analytical assumptions. This research strongly connects Indian education with European structural standards.

Overflow Detection in Drainage Systems

This project addressed urban flooding by detecting overflow conditions in drainage systems. Sensors were used to monitor water levels and trigger alerts during abnormal flow conditions.

The project focused on practical problem solving, system integration, and presentation of engineering ideas. It received second prize in a technical poster presentation.

Seminar on Sustainable Construction Materials

This seminar studied the use of recycled aggregates, bamboo, timber, and other renewable resources in building construction. The focus was on material properties, environmental benefits, and limitations in structural applications.

The seminar improved my understanding of sustainability in civil engineering and the importance of alternative materials in future construction practices.

Extensive Survey Project

As part of an extensive field survey project, I worked on highway alignment, water supply systems, old and new tank surveys, and town planning layouts.

The project involved total station surveying, data processing, drawing preparation, and interpretation of field measurements. This experience strengthened my practical understanding of surveying and planning.

Industry Internship at Medini Technologies

Completed a fifteen week internship focused on AutoCAD drafting, BIM workflows, drawing standards, and professional documentation practices.
