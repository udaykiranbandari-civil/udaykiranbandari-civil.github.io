Research, Academic Projects and Internship

From Indian Standards to Eurocodes

This independent research focused on comparing reinforced concrete beam design using IS 456 and Eurocode 2. The study examined differences in safety philosophy, partial safety factors, and serviceability requirements.

Overflow Detection in Drainage Systems

A sensor based project addressing urban drainage overflow problems. The system focused on early detection and monitoring of overflow conditions. This project was presented as a technical poster and received second prize.

Extensive Survey Project

A large scale field survey project covering old tank survey, new tank survey, highway alignment, town planning layout, and water supply systems. The project involved detailed data collection, processing, and final drawings.

Industry Internship
